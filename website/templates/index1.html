  • About Models


    Our acne grading website utilizes two advanced deep learning models: ResNet-50 and DenseNet-169. Both models are renowned for their robust performance and have been pre-trained on extensive datasets to leverage their powerful feature extraction capabilities.
    To ensure our models are highly accurate and reliable, we trained them on a diverse dataset of over 1000 images. These images included both original and augmented versions to enhance the models' ability to generalize across various acne presentations.
    Augmentation techniques such as random cropping, horizontal flipping, color jittering, and histogram equalization were applied to simulate a wide range of real-world conditions and improve the models' robustness.

    Model Performance


    ResNet-50: Achieved an impressive 83% training accuracy and 80% validation accuracy.
    DenseNet-169: Reached an outstanding 85.5% training accuracy and 77% validation accuracy.

    These accuracies reflect our commitment to providing reliable and consistent acne severity assessments.
    The use of both original and augmented images has played a crucial role in enhancing the models' performance, allowing them to effectively analyze and classify acne severity from diverse and complex image inputs.
    Our models are designed for research and informational purposes, providing a solid foundation for further advancements in automated acne assessment.
